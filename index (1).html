Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Artistry

Founded in 1987 by Shri Ramesh Kumar in the heart of Indore, Krishna Jewellers began as a modest workshop with a profound belief: that every piece of jewellery should carry a soul.

Three decades later, we remain a family-run establishment, now in its second generation. Our craftsmen — many of whom have been with us for over 20 years — blend traditional Rajasthani and Mughal techniques with contemporary sensibility.
